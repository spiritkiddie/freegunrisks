Who We Are

Its undoubtedly true that the African continent is witnessing an economic renaissance, and therefore Africa as an investment destination is very compelling, but the higher level of the unknown risk within the investment space still remains a key fundamental issue.

Fregun Risk Advisors Ltd (FRA)is an independent risk management firm that focuses on providing top down, bottom up risk advisory services to Companies and Investors who seek to access Investment opportunities across the West Africa countries of Ghana, Ivory Coast, and Togo.

Our advisory services specialize on selected West Africa countries and industry sectors with the aim of providing our clients with tailor-made services.

At core of FRAservices is our commitment to assisting our clients to navigate the uncertainties with a risk advisory services based on the valves of trust, integrity and unbiased advice.

We are led by experience senior professional advisors and anchored with a network of expertise across West Africa and Europe.

Quick info

  • FRA founded in UK 2015
  • 2016 established our subsidiaries in Ghana and Austria

The Ghana subsidiary is the business hub for Ghana, Ivory Coast and Togo, our UK and Austria subsidiary focuses on the three German speaking countries Austria, Switzerland & Germany.
