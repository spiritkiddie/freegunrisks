Our Case Studies

Case Study for our VC Client

Client PE/VC firm in New York
Scenario Ghana based micro financing firm was looking for capital for expansion to become and Loans and Savings Bank. This means the micro financing institution is seeking to become a fully Licenced Bank in Ghana.
Clients Request After receiving a presentation from a corporate finance advisory firm based in Ghana, FRA was to review the Risk management proposal as well as identify all other areas of risk for recommendation.
Our Findings
  • We made the VC firm aware that the micro financing sectors in Ghana are going through a very difficult period at the moment because of regulatory constraints. Regulations by the Bank of Ghana on the micro financing sectors remain ambiguous and are not clear.
  • The VC was aware of the very low political risk as well as high levels of FX depreciation of the local currency for sometime now.
  • FRA raised a number of concerns with Operational risk which was not addressed by the firm.
Outcome Our VC client needed to fully understand the operational risks involved and how the micro finance firm are going to manage other areas of its risk exposures.
