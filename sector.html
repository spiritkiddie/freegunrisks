Focus Sectors

Our advisory services specializes on selected countries and industry sectors. We believe that businesses and investors are no longer looking at Africa as a whole for investment opportunities, Africa is now been viewed on a selected individual countries macroeconomic fundamentals and good governance.

At FRA our belief is to focus on individual countries and industry sectors which we know best, this means our resources and experience are channeled to areas that we can best assist our clients. Our sectors of interest are as follows:

  • Financial Service
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Agribusiness
  • Real Estate
Africa is witnessing unwavering business and investment opportunities across the Continent.
