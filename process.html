Our Process

At FRA our commitment is simply to our clients, by taking holistic approach to risk assessment rather than the silo approach, we leverage on our exceptional expertise who are at the forefront across the countries and sectors we cover to provide our clients with risk intelligence services based on Identify, Analyze and Evaluate.

Risk Identify

Our process of identification starts with an in-depth conversation to understand the interest of our clients and how best we can assist to identify and migrate any risks. Upon this conversation, we combine our human intelligence and rigorous information gathering process, which formed the bases for our risk assessment.

Risk Analyze

Given the complexity and sometimes the extreme nature of risk associated for accessing investment opportunities across Africa has meant that, our analysis process combines analytical methods and scenario analysis that formed an integral part to ask and answer the “what if“ questions from our clients. We strongly believe that, one size never fits all when it come to risk appetite and assessment, which means every country and every industry sectors are analyze individually.

Evaluate

With our risk identification and analysis reports, we work with our clients to fully evaluate and understand the risks they are likely to be faced with and how to best manage all risks.

Looking across the African continent for investment opportunities, having an early upfront understanding of the challenges and ground realities of the business environment across the Continent is very important.
