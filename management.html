Our people

Fregun Risk Advisors Ltd is headed by Managing Director Fred Nowacki, and anchor with a network of exceptional advisors and consultants team across West Africa and the EU.

Prior to forming FRA, Mr. Nowacki was a Risk Manager at Sberbank Europe AG in Vienna,Austria, where he was a senior member of the Vienna operational, market and Liquidity risk team that set the directions for all market risks for Sberbank Europe AG operations across the CEE Sberbank Group.

Before joining Sberbank Europe AG, Mr. Nowacki worked for WestLB AG and HSBC in London as a market and operational risk manager. At HSBC Mr. Nowacki was a key member for building the HSBC customer activity monitoring Program(CAMP) which is used across HSBC worldwide for Anti money laundering process.

Mr. Nowacki began his career with Merrill Lynch Private Wealth Management in New York.

Mr . Nowacki received an Master of Science in Financial Risk Management and postgraduate diploma in International Finance from London Metropolitan University and a BA in Banking from the London Guildhall University/City University of New York.(CUNY)
